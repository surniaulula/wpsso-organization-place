Automated Removal

  1. Go to the admin section of your WordPress site.
  2. Select the Plugins menu item.
  3. Select the Installed Plugins sub-menu.
  4. Scroll down to the "WPSSO Organization Markup" plugin (or use the Search field), and click its Deactivate link.
  5. Scroll down to the "WPSSO Organization Markup" plugin (or use the Search field), and click its Delete link.
  6. Click on the Yes, Delete these files and data button.

Manual Removal

  1. Remove the wordpress/wp-content/plugins/wpsso-organization/ folder from your website.
